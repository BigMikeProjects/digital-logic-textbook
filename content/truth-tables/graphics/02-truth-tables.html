Sum of Products form
Hover a product term, or press Build to fill the table one term at a time.
The big idea
Each product (AND) term says exactly where the 1s go.
A row is 1 if any product term is true — the terms are OR-ed together.
Product of Sums form
Hover a sum term, or press Build to knock out the zeros one term at a time.
The big idea
Each sum (OR) term says exactly where the 0s go.
A row is 0 if any sum term is false — the terms are AND-ed together.
Same function, same F column. Every 1 is owned by a product term; every 0 is owned by a sum term.
